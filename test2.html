Definition of plants

  • - Plants are multicellular living organisms that belong to the plant kingdom; their cells contain a nucleus and chloroplasts.
  • - It carries out photosynthesis to convert sunlight and carbon dioxide into food (glucose).
  • - There are more than 300,000 plant species on the Earth's surface.
